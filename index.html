Edge Orientation Ledge

This is typically achieved by doing a first block on bottom left, followed by 3/4 of the belt, and orienting all edges while inserting the last belt edge (EOLE).

6 Corners' Orientation

After achieving EO-ledge, there are 6 corners remaining on the cube. These corners are to be oriented with one of 71 short algoritms.

6 Corners' Permutation

Next, the 6 corners are permuted using one of 47 algorithms, and offsetting the D layer by a D or a D' (or rotating) so that the final step can be MU-gen.

Last 5 Edges' Permutation

Finally, the last 5 edges are permuted using one of 16 short MU-gen algorithms. The U and the D layers may be need to be aligned at this point.

Movecount: 45-50

Algorithms: 134 + 55
